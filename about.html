The Story

Why we built Tiles WMS

It started with a visit to a tiles distributor in Pune. Their warehouse manager showed us 14 different Excel files — one per product category — all updated manually by hand. Every day, 2 staff members spent 3 hours just reconciling stock numbers across sheets.

They knew they had a problem. They'd tried generic inventory software — but it didn't understand tiles. It didn't know about rack storage by size, or GST invoicing with HSN codes, or the complexity of managing Italian imports alongside domestic stock in the same warehouse.

So we talked to 40 more tiles businesses across India. The same story, over and over. Manual processes, expensive errors, GST headaches, and no visibility across locations.

So we built Tiles WMS. Not a generic warehouse tool. A system purpose-built for the way tiles businesses actually work — rack-level storage, GST-first invoicing, multi-warehouse visibility, and a UI that warehouse staff can actually use.
